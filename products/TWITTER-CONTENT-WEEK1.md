## Evergreen Tweets (post anytime)

### Tip tweets:

Your AI prompts suck because you're missing one thing: constraints.

"Write a tagline" → generic output
"Write a tagline under 6 words using a verb, addressing [problem], avoiding 'innovative' and 'best'" → gold

More constraints = more creative AI.

---

The #1 automation every small business should set up:

Auto-draft email replies.

Takes 30 minutes to set up with Zapier + ChatGPT.
Saves 3-5 hours per week.

You still review every email. But instead of writing from scratch, you're editing pre-written drafts.

---

Stop asking ChatGPT "Write me a blog post."

Start asking:
"Act as a content strategist specializing in SEO. Create a blog outline for [topic] targeting [keyword] for [audience]. Include 5-7 H2 sections, introduction hook, and CTA."

The difference? Specificity.
